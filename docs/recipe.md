RECIPE: CAMEMBERT WITH SOUR FIG

# Serves: 6-8

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cooking time: 10 minutes (optional)

Occasion: Easy weekday, Effortless entertaining, Shameless snacking

# Ingredients

1 jar of Sour Fig Jam  
1 camembert cheese
crackers or ciabatta

# Method

    Remove the camembert cheese from the fridge and allow it to soften at room temperature.

    Alternatively, place the cheese in a small oven-proof dish and bake at 180 °C for 10 minutes. 

    Smother the baked or unbaked camembert with Sour Fig Jam and smear lavishly on crackers or ciabatta.
